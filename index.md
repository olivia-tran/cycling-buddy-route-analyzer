## FAQ
### How do I analyze a route?
Paste a public Strava route URL on the home screen and tap **Analyze**. You’ll get total distance, elevation gain, the toughest climbs, and any steep descents.

### How do I compare two routes?
Paste a second route URL in the optional field and tap **Analyze Route(s)**. You’ll see a side‑by‑side comparison for distance, elevation, climbs, and descents.

### How do I share a route or comparison?
On a single route, tap **Share this ride with friends?**. For two routes, tap **Share comparison** to send both links and a summary.

### Does the app require a Strava login?
No. Cycling Buddy uses publicly available route information and does not require a Strava login.
